Fully customize your OSD ( On Screen Display ) pop ups like Volume , Brightness or Caffeine , Lock Keys etc. Move, orient, resize, numeric %, choose monitor, set hide-delay and style to match your theme and liking. You can also select any combination of components to display: icon, text-label, level-bar, numeric %.
